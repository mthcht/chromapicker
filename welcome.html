Welcome - let's pin Chromapicker to your toolbar

Pinning makes the color picker one click away from every web page. Takes five seconds.

Quick setup
1
Find the extensions menu Click the puzzle piece icon in the top-right of your browser.
2
Find Chromapicker in the list Look for Chromapicker - Color Picker & Mixer among your installed extensions.
3
Click the pin icon Toggle the small pushpin icon so it turns accent-colored. That's it - Chromapicker now lives in your toolbar.
